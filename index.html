Software Engineer (3 months Internship)

Aberdeen's Robert Gordon University (Scotland) | 2016

As an intern my mission was to develop (from scratch) a prototype Android and Android wear app as part of a project that intended to develop a support system for people living with low back pain. The app was collecting data from the sensors present on a smartwatch, sending it on a mobile phone where the data was analysed in order to predict the activity done by the user, and then the result was displayed. The most important part was to implement the activity recognition system developped by a research fellow. I worked alone and independently, however, as I was creating a prototype destined to other people, I made sure that to have a clean and well documented code.

  • Java Android, for mobile phone and smartwatch
  • Librairies: OpenCV and MPAndroidChart
